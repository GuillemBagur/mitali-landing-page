FAQs

Here we will answer some of the most common Mitali related questions. If you dont find your answer here, please contact us, we are happy to help you!

What is Mitali?

It's an app to show all of your trophies to the Internet. Your profile will be linked to an URL, so you can share it with whoever you want.

Is Mitali free?

Of course! We will keep a completely free plan forever. In the future, maybe we add some extra features, for premium users. But, if you create your account now, you'll have access to all the features for free forever!

How do you get all the trophies info?

We use your real name to automatically search into sports federation websites, in search of your performances. If we miss any important trophy, you can contact us to claim it.

My identity is already taken, what should I do?

If someone has used your name, you can claim that shelf. Please, contact us.

Do you have a paid plan?

At the moment, no. But we're planning to add some extra features for users who want the best.
